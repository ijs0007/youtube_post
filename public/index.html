How It Works

Magic Marquee uploads a finished film straight to cloud storage, then transfers it to YouTube as a private draft by default (change the visibility, category, tags & more under Upload options). If the film has dialogue, it auto-transcribes and writes the title, description & chapters for you to review (or add your own transcript to skip that). Large files upload in chunks automatically. Last step: make a thumbnail.

Part of the Magic Suite. Marquee is one of four connected apps that share your login and project data: Story Maker (write the script, build cast & crew) · Reel (share footage for selects) · Marquee (this app — thumbnails & YouTube upload) · Credits (end-credit roll). Switch between them from the links up top — sign in once and your projects follow you across all four.
